## What I Do

I sit at the intersection of **product thinking** and **data analysis** — finding the numbers behind decisions, then turning them into stories that drive action.

```
Raw Data  →  Cleaned Pipeline  →  Statistical Analysis  →  Visual Insight  →  Product Decision
```

- **Find the signal** — clean, transform, and engineer features from messy real-world datasets
- **Frame the problem** — define KPIs, build hypotheses, and stress-test assumptions with statistics  
- **Build the story** — Tableau dashboards and structured narratives that non-technical stakeholders act on
- **Use AI as leverage** — accelerate analysis, surface hidden patterns, generate first-draft recommendations

### Rapido Revenue Leak Analysis

[![Rapido Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Project-Rapido%20Revenue%20Leak%20Analysis-FFCC00?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=000000)](https://github.com/vedant-valid/rapido-revenue-leak-analysis)
[![Tableau Dashboard](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live%20Dashboard-Tableau%20Public-E97627?style=for-the-badge&logo=tableau&logoColor=white)](https://public.tableau.com/views/Rapido_Rides_Analysis_17780461220670/Overview?:language=en-US&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)

</div>

> Analysed **49,801 Bangalore ride transactions** to identify where Rapido bleeds revenue through driver-supply mismatches and peak-hour cancellations.

| Finding | Impact |
|:---|:---|
| 10.4% cancellation rate across 50K rides | ₹6.8M in revenue put at risk |
| +15–20% cancel spike during peak hours | `cab_economy` & `auto` hit hardest |
| Top 5 zones: 10% cancel reduction | **₹1,20,000/month recovered** |
| Koramangala + HSR Layout | 40% of total revenue, inconsistent supply |

**Stack:** Python · Pandas · SciPy · Tableau Public · Jupyter
